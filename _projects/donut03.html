About the project

This project development intends to create an interface, for both mobile and desktop aiming to promote and sell a specific product.

The methodologies used seek to develop a practical, intuitive solution and provide a positive experience for the user

My role: UI and UX Designer

Challenge and objective

The challenge was to select a physical product and design an online store that only sells that product, to create an online donut shop that contains the various interfaces associated with the product itself.

Process

Analysis and Discovery

To create this app I had to develop from scratch a Design System, a comprehensive guide for project design – a collection of rules, principles, constraints and best practices. From buttons to grids, to colors and sizes. All of the parts of the System are crucial for the visual design.

The designs for both mobile and desktop play a fundamental role in structuring information allowing you to generate new ideas in a fast and free way.

Final Soluction

With the development of the final mockup we can see the color schemes, content layouts, fonts, icons, navigation visuals, images, and the overall feeling of the future software product design and user experience. It’s really important to see the work coming to life. I tried to create mockups that are...

  • Convincing
  • Realistic
  • Easy to revise

And also they offer a great opportunity to study the results of the design decisions made previously.
